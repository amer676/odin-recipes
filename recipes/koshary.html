Koshary

Ready to experience the famous egyptian dish?

Koshary is pretty much Egypt's staple street food. You can find anything from a 2-story koshary restaurant to a man with a cart on the side of the street. It has no meat, but even the biggest of carnivores don't miss it.

Ingredients

  • chickpeas
  • ground coriander and cayenne pepper
  • ditalini pasta
  • rinsed rice
  • cold water
  • brown lentils
  • salt and black pepper
  • olive oil
  • onion and garlic
  • tomato
  • butter
  • chicken stock
  • french fried onions

Instructions

  1. Combine components.
  2. Bring a pot of water to a boil and cook pasta.
  3. combine rice with cold water.
  4. combine lentils, season them and bring to a boild.
  5. heat olive oil, garlice and onion add the tomatoes and season.
  6. melt butter add chicken stock and season.
  7. mix rice and lentil together spreak the pasta serve with sauce, chikpeas and french fried onions
